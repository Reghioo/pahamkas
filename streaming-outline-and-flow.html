STREAMING: OUTLINE AND FLOW

Our streaming episode is designed with a minimum of 30 minutes and a maximum of 1 Â½ hours. During the stream, we will optimize viewer engagement and content depth which will be handled by the hosts who will be in charge of the overall streaming episode. In addition, the host will extract insights, experiences, and potential solutions from our guests who are a prominent user of Facebook.

To further enhance audience participation, we have incorporated a raffle part in our streaming plan which will have 5 winners of 100 pesos each. This deliberate incentive not only amplifies engagement but also encourages active interaction, making our streaming experience not just informative but also an inclusive and rewarding journey for our audience.
