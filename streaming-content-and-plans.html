I. STREAM OVERVIEW

The “Pahamkas: Isiwalat ang Boses at Talino – Podcast #1” will feature an interview-focused discussion centered on the theme of "Facebook Utilization during the Pandemic and Its Impact on the Academics of Grade 10 Students.

II. OBJECTIVES

A team of five is dedicated to assisting learners in gaining a comprehensive understanding of how social media, specifically Facebook, can impact one's lifestyle and educational standing. Through an insightful podcast, the team aims to disseminate their latest research findings concerning the challenges associated with Facebook usage. The podcast will serve as a platform to share valuable insights on strategies to address and mitigate the impact of Facebook on individuals. The approach will be interactive, incorporating facts and real-life situations from both the past and present, featuring guest students who will contribute their perspectives and experiences to enrich the educational discourse.

III. EXPECTATIONS AND TARGETS

As the livestream concludes, we anticipate that the audience will gain enhanced social media literacy, facilitated by engaging interactions with the streamers. This will include the sharing of opinions and responses to viewer-submitted questions. Our goal is to maintain a viewership of at least 50 individuals throughout the stream, which is expected to last between a minimum of 30 minutes and a maximum of 1 ½ hours. Lastly, here are the expected topics to be covered in our stream:

These are the expected coverage of the stream:

- COVID-19 Experience (specifically in school)

- Academic Relationship (i.e. peers, classmates, friends)

- Facebook Utilization (advantages and disadvantages)

IV. RESOURCE PLAN

Throughout the live stream, we will be conducting a raffle, offering a 100 Pesos incentive to the audience to enhance connectivity and foster interaction. A budget of 500 Pesos has been allocated for the stream, with prizes awarded to five lucky winners selected through the raffle.

V. KEY PERSONS

In each podcast episode, every team member will shoulder specific responsibilities within the framework of four designated departments: design, production, technical, and marketing. These are the roles and commitments of each member:

Design Department

Shannon Alisson Joyosa will lead the design department, with support from Ghiger Yoh Abril and Ataliah Ysabelle Dalere. This team is tasked with creating all materials essential to the stream's production, including the streaming layout.

Production Department

Ataliah Ysabelle Dalere and Princess Asiana Macalaindong will lead the production department, with assistance from the rest of the team. They are responsible for overseeing all content and production aspects of each podcast episode, including selecting initial guests and raffle winners.

Technical Department

Ghiger Yoh Abril will lead the technical department, assisted by Shannon Alisson Joyosa, manage the technical aspects of each episode and provide support in case of any streaming malfunctions. They play a critical role in ensuring a smooth streaming connection and tracking viewership.

Marketing Department

Jakob Willem Recalde will lead the marketing department, supported by the entire team. This team oversees the creation of promotional materials and facilitates the team's connectivity with followers.

For this specific episode, Ghiger Yoh Abril and Ataliah Ysabelle Dalere will serve as the main hosts. Given the team's newness to streaming, their role is crucial in ensuring viewer engagement and interaction. Additionally, they will be joined by two previous 10th-grade students from APEC Schools Ortigas Extension as a guest.
